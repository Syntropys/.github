# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume good intent
- Give constructive feedback
- Follow community and GitHub guidelines

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks, threats, or trolling
- Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content, restrict access, or ban users for violations.

## Reporting
Report issues by opening an issue in this repository.
